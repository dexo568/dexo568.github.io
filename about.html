DECLAN MCKELVEY-HEMBREE
Maker. Programmer. Designer.

Hey! I'm Declan. Originally from Los Angeles, I am currently working at Fusion Marketing in St. Louis as a application developer.

When I'm not working on marketing gimmicks, I'm usually making video games, interactive art, or robots. I think how humans interact with technology is incredibly interesting, and although I think the world has largely settled on functional UX design, there's a whole world of interesting alternate interaction models to explore.

I also spend a great deal of time mentoring computer science students, as teaching in general is something that I find incredibly fulfilling. I don't pretend to be an expert programmer, but helping people achieve introductory-level professional competence is something I find incredibly satisfying, and probably the most worthwhile thing I do.
